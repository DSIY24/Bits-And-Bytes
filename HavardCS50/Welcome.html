Welcome

Welcome to my blog! Here, I share my notes and insights on the Harvard CS50 Introduction to Computer Science course offered on edX.

As an enthusiast and learner, not a professional, my goal is to provide a supplementary resource for those navigating this fascinating subject. It's essential to note that while I strive for accuracy and clarity in my notes, they should not replace the comprehensive materials provided directly by Harvard.

Instead, think of them as a helpful summary and personal perspective on each lesson. I encourage you to use these notes in conjunction with the course materials to enhance your understanding and learning experience.

Join me on this journey through the fundamentals of computer science, and let's delve into the exciting world of CS50 together!
