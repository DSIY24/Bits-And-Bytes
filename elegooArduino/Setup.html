Welcome

Welcome to my blog, where I'll guide you through the Elegoo Super Starter Kit and the world of Arduino. While the official guide PDF is a great starting point, it often lacks depth in explaining how and why things work. Here, I'll provide clearer insights into the Arduino Uno board and its software, helping both beginners and those looking to deepen their knowledge. Join me as we explore not just how to build projects, but also the principles behind them!

In this Setup page I will go through Lesson 0 and 1 which goes through installing the Arduino Integrated Development Environment (IDE) and the libaries that are needed.

Frist we have to install the Arduino Integrated Development Environment (IDE)

The Arduino software that you will use to program the Elegoo board is available for Windows, Mac and Linux. The installation process is different for all three platforms, In this setup lesson I will go through the installation process for windows. If you have a mac or Linux you can follow the office Elegoo Super Starter Kit PDF which is linked in the sidebar of this website.

Frist we have to go to https://www.arduino.cc/en/software and click on the operating system that you are using, In most cases for a newer window laptop the Windows 10 and newer 64 bit is the right choice

Then click on Just Download

You should then click on I agree in the following prompt

Click next to proceed

And click install

Once the installation is complete then see the following interface, click on install
